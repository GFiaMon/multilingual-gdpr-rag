If a participant accidentally shares confidential health data during a recorded online training session, what are my GDPR breach notification obligations as the data controller? What if the automated recording system stores this sensitive data in cloud processing?

--- Message 2 ---
Time: 2025-10-17T09:57:41Z
Role: assistant

As the data controller, if a participant accidentally shares confidential health data during a recorded online training session, you are required to notify the affected individuals without undue delay according to Article 34(1) of the GDPR. Additionally, you must document this breach in your records and may need to conduct a data protection impact assessment if the data use was deemed risky. Failure to comply with these obligations could result in fines of up to €10 million or 2% of your annual global turnover.
